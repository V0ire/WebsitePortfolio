Do I Look Like Him?

Support This Game

Haunted by too many traumas from his past, a young man dedicates his life to proving he’s nothing like his abusive, drug-addicted father. He pushes himself to exhaustion, drowning in academics, overloading on volunteer work, desperate to erase every but of him in his soul. But when burnout consumes him and isolation takes hold, he’s forced to face a chilling truth. Will he break the cycle, or will the past devour him?

 "Do I Look Like Him?" is a psychological horror, narrative-driven experience where every choice pulls you deeper into the mind of a man on the edge. Can you stop the descent?
